History
The Strong Believer
Abū Hurayrah (may Allah be pleased with him) reported that the Messenger of Allah ﷺ said: “The strong believer is better and dearer to Allah than the weak believer, and there is good in both. Adhere to whatever brings you benefit, seek the help of Allah, and do not feel helpless. If something befalls you, do not say: ‘Had I done such-and-such, it would have been such-and-such;’ rather, say: ‘Allah has decreed, and whatever He wills, He does.’ Indeed, ‘if’ opens the way before the devil to act.”
